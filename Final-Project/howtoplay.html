Introduction

Welcome to CLASS-DASH! In this game you will play as Dr. Marshall—an IT professor at Brigham Young University–Hawaii. Dr. Marshall is late to teach his class, but there are piles of broken hardware and chatty professors in his way. To win, you must help Dr. Marshall avoid broken servers, PCs, professors, and the local Mynah bird migration so that he can make it to class on time!

Controls

This game has only two controls. Use the up-arrow to jump and the down arrow to duck.
