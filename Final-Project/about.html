Purpose

This game is meant to instill preliminary interest in information technology and higher education for adolescent children and teens. By introducing potentially foreign tools/vocabulary such as "servers", "professors", "PCs" and more, the game encourages young users to seek out information through parents, mentors, or additional resources provided at the bottom of this page.
