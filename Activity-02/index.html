Cats: The Movie that Made Us Question It All

My Favorite Reviews

  • "At best, it’s an ambitious misfire. At worst, it’s straight-up nightmare fuel that will haunt generations. Enter into the world of the Jellicles at your own peril."
    -Daniel Howat, Next Best Picture
  • "I finally understand that one episode of Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt."
    -Cate Young
  • "No disrespect to people who love this musical, this is the worst movie of the year... next year too."
    -Lonita Cook, KCTV5 at 9
  • "Dragging on for almost two hours of poor singing and predominantly forgettable choreography, Cats is Hooper's way of maliciously toying with us like a half-dead mouse wishing for sweet release."

  • "It is an oddity all to itself, a spectacular disaster in every sense of the term. It's a drug-induced hallucination brought to life."
    -Allen Almachar
  • "Oh God, my eyes"
    -Ty Burr, Substack
  • "It's one of those rare cinematic events that feels like a collective hallucination - improbable and entirely indescribable."
    -Clarisse Loughry
  • Cats absolutely does commit to every choice it made. I would like to respect it for that, but the choices were bad.
    -Eve Tushnet, Patheos
  • "For all of the star power on display, this is an indoor Cats: spayed, defanged and thoroughly domesticated."
    -Ty Burr, Substack
  • "I, for one, felt lost, grappling down a sketchy back alley for rhyme, reason and, once the credits rolled, the nearest exit."
    -Candace McMillan, Seattle Refined
